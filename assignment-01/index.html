My Top 5 Video Games

These are five of my favorite video games based on gameplay, creativity, and entertainment.

  1. Grand Theft Auto V

    Grand Theft Auto V has a large open world, an interesting story, and many different activities to complete.

  2. NBA 2K

    NBA 2K allows players to build their own basketball player, compete online, and play as real NBA teams.

  3. Fortnite

    Fortnite combines building, shooting, and survival gameplay in competitive matches with other players.

  4. Minecraft

    Minecraft gives players the freedom to build, explore, collect resources, and create their own worlds.

  5. Call of Duty

    Call of Duty provides fast-paced action, multiplayer competition, and several different game modes.
